    Undying of the Martyred Saint¶

    As a chosen one of the Martyred Saint, a disciple, an incarnation, a monk, or a templar, when you die violently at the hands of Saint’s enemies, you erupt from your shallow grave at sunrise and seek vengeance. You are now a revenant.

    Stats¶

    Upon your resurrection, you lose your former identity but gain supernatural endurance.

    • Lose one level.
    • Erase your character’s name, bonds, and drives.
    • Lose all your class and heritage moves. You gain new revenant class moves described in the following sections.
    • Add 10 to your maximum HP.
    • Take the first Degradation (see the following section).

    Drive¶

    You now have only one burning desire: Vengence on the enemies of your Saint!

    Undying¶

    Your mortal shell has only a tenuous link to your soul.

    • You do not breathe, eat, drink, or sleep.
    • You also do not heal as usual. You gain no benefit from poultices and herbs, bandages, magical healing, make camp, or recovery.
    • You are immune to all effects that turn or control undead.

    Degradation¶

    When you become a revenant, take the first of the following six degradations. Add the subsequent degradation whenever the result of a move instructs you to do so. Degredations manifest in the order listed, and each is selected only once.

    # Degradation Details
    1 Strange Appetite Choose one particular type of sustenance:
    • Bone and marrow
    • Brains
    • Hearts
    • Still-warm blood
    You heal a quarter of your HP or remove one debility whenever you consume the sustenance. Your strange appetite is the only way to restore your HP or remove debilities.

    Whenever you have the opportunity to indulge your strange appetite, you lose all control and immediately begin to gorge, oblivious to your surroundings, unless you succeed in a Defy Danger roll +WIS.
    2 Carrion Stench A horrible odor always follows you. Natural beasts shy away from you or become aggressive.
    3 Dark Vision You can see even in absolute darkness. Sunlight pains you. You take -1 ongoing in sunlight.
    4 Deathly Visage It’s clear to all who look at you that you are dead. When you use intimidation and your sinister appearance to Parley, you gain +1 forward.
    5 Home to Vermin Insects have taken up residence in your corpse. These bugs will do favors if you ask. Treat them as Followers (Quality +0, Loyalty +0, 1 HP, meek, stealthy, swarm, tiny).
    6 The Final Degradation Your soul’s tenuous link to your mortal shell is severed for eternity. Your body spontaneously turns to ash and blows away on an unfelt breeze. Your soul returns to your Saint. Roll a new character.

    Starting Moves¶

    Upon becoming a revenant, you gain the following moves.

    Move Description
    Forgotten Former Life Your overriding drive is now simply Revenge and Retribution for the enemies of your Saint—nothing else. Add this question to your Discern Realities list:
    ”What is the quickest way to my vengence?”
    When you act on the answer, gain +1 forward and mark XP.
    From the Underworld Returned You bear a mark of your time in the underworld. Choose a part of your body that takes on the visible aspects of death while remaining fully functional:
    • Arm: Any weapon you grasp flows into the underworld. It gains +2 piercing against living creatures.
    • Eye: When you touch a corpse, you see what it saw in its last moments. The GM will describe it.
    • Legs: Your passing leaves no mark or scent as a living person would.
    Inexorable When you push the limits of your unliving body, choose one of the following outcomes to attempt.
    • Perform a feat of inhuman strength.
    • Move with uncanny speed and grace.
    • Refuse to be moved, restrained, or knocked off your feet.
    Roll +CON:
    • On a 10+, suffer d4 damage (no armor reduction), and the attempt succeeds.
    • On 7–9, suffer d6 damage (no armor reduction), and the attempt succeeds.
    • On a miss, suffer d8 damage (no armor reduction), the attempt fails, and you suffer any other consequences of your missed roll.
    Mark of the Undead Dead spirits see your mark and know you as their own. Any dead spirit still clinging to the mortal plane can speak with you. The living, especially the devout, see your mark as a sign of sin. You take a -1 ongoing to CHA rolls with the living who see your mark.
    Sustained by Rage Neither poison nor disease harms you. You feel no pain. When you take damage from weapons, take half damage (rounded up, after armor reduction).

    When you are reduced to zero HP, roll +CON.
    • On a 10+, regain half your HP and choose one effect from the following list.
    • On a 7–9, regain a quarter of your HP and choose two effects.
    • On a miss, regain 1 HP, and all three effects apply.
    Effects
    • Add the next Degradation (see the previous section).
    • You’re out of the action and incapacitated for d6 hours or until sunrise (whichever is shorter).
    • Your body is permanently disfigured in some way. Describe it.
    Note:
    • If your body is destroyed (for example, burned to ash), treat it as a roll of 6- and your soul lingers incapacitated until your mortal shell reforms with 1 HP at the next sunrise.

    Advanced Moves¶

    You may pick from the following moves starting at level two. After selecting both moves, you gain no new moves when leveling up until level six.

    Move Description
    Branded Requires From the Underworld Returned
    Choose a second body part to take on the visible aspects of death.
    Indelible Requires From the Underworld Returned
    The underworld’s mark grows so that it may not be concealed. Describe it. The dead will seek you out, giving you what aid they can and not harm you. The devout will treat you as a heretic.

    Master Moves¶

    You may pick from the following moves starting at level six. After selecting both moves, you gain no new moves when leveling up.

    Move Description
    Blemished Requires Branded
    Choose a third body part to take on the visible aspects of death.
    Whispers in the Dark When you speak to a dead spirit, you take +1 forward with all who knew that spirit in life.

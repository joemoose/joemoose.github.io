Offworld Colonization

From 2150, hyperdrive starships have been travelling out from Earth to colonize, settle, explore and exploit the strange new worlds around alien suns. Today, in 2250, there are over 300 surveyed planets in the interstellar space surrounding Earth. A ring of stars surrounds Earth at a distance of around 4 parsecs or so and these were the target systems for the first interstellar colonies They have continued to develop and grow ever since. The competing power blocs of Earth used hyperspace to explore and Settle areas of space in different directions to create quite discrete (political) sectors of settlement.

The three sectors are the American, the European (or 'German') and the Japanese.

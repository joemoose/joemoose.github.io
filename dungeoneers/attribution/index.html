    Attribution¶

    I stand on the shoulders of giants.

    Dungeoneers builds upon the trailblazing creativity of Yochai Gal’s Cairn, Chris McDowall’s Into the ODD, Ben Milton’s Knave, Isaac Williams’ Mausritter, and the resulting minimalist OSR and NSR diaspora.

    • Archetypes starting gear adapted from “d66 Cairn Background Loadouts”, d66 Classless Kobolds, 12 January 2021, by Jim Parkin.

    • Carousing adapted from Cairn Adventurer’s Guide by Adam Hensley and “Party like it's 999”, Jeff’s Gameblog, 19 December 2008, by Jeff Rients.

    • Corruption adapted from Weird North by Jim Parkin.

    • Drives adapted from “The Grey Shaded Hex” by Lord Simon Black, KNOCK #2, published by The Merry Mushmen and Kamchatka Publishing, pp. 70-71, and used with permission.

    • Equipment adapted from Dungeon World, by Adam Koebel and Sage LaTorra.

    • Keepsakes adapted from “When All You Have is a Hammer”, Against the Wicked City, 4 February 2018, by Joseph Manola.

    • Luck mechanism adapted from Spellburn and Battlescars by Luís Fernando Anizello and Matheus Henrique Morais.

    • Physical mutations adapted from Vaults of Vaarn #1 by Leo Hunt.

    • Sorcerer class adapted from “OSR: Class: Sorcerer”, Coins and Scrolls, 29 March 2018, by Skerples.

    • Spell system adapted from “The GLOG”, Goblin Punch, 7 May 2016, by Arnold Kemp and “D100 - Potential Spell Recharge Conditions”, d4 Caltrops, 19 September 2022, by Ktrey.

    • "Success, Partial Success, Failure" dice mechanic adapted from Apocalypse World by Meguey and Vincent Baker.

    • Sundering of shields adapted from “Shields Shall be Splintered!”, Trollsmyth, 6 May 2008, by Trollsmyth.

    • Supply chests adapted from “Sack Packs”, Shoalmont Games, 28 November 2018, by ATXRedBeard.

    • Usage dice mechanic adapted from The Black Hack SRD by Jack Black.

    • Warrior and Thief classes adapted from The Electrum Archive, Issue #01 by Emiel Boen.

    • Zones adapted from the Fate System Toolkit, a product of Evil Hat Productions, LLC, developed, authored, and edited by Robert Donoghue, Brian Engard, Brennan Taylor, Mike Olson, Mark Diaz Truman, Fred Hicks, and Matthew Gandy, and licensed for our use under the Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported license.
